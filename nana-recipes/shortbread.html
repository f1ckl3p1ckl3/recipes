Shortbread

from Mum

INGREDIENTS:

  • 250 g butter
  • 4 ozs castor sugar
  • 2 ozs ground rice
  • 12 ozs plain flour
  • pinch of salt

METHOD:

  1. Have the butter soft like in the summer
  2. Stir in all the sugar, then the ground rice and plain flour (a bit at a time)
  3. Mix well
  4. Make dough into a large ball and divide into 6 portions
  5. Roll or pat into 6 circles and run knife through to divide circles into 6 pieces
  6. Crimp edges and stick fork twice into each piece
  7. Cook at 140 C (if fan forced, cook 3 trays together, one on each rung for 30 mins... if not, cook at 150 C 1 tray at a time)
  8. Cut immediately into pieces when taken from oven. Lift carefully on to a cake rack when cool and leave to go cold. Store shortbread in airtight container

Notes:

  • "With a fork, poke holes in shortbread just before oven" - Annabel :)
