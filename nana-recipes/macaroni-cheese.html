Macaroni Cheese

Serves 5 (from Barb)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 3 cups macaroni, cooked
  • 6 rashers bacon, diced and fried
  • 2 onions, diced and fried
  • Punnet cherry tomatoes
  • 12 pitted black olives
  • handful chopped parsley (optional)
  • 1/2 lb grated cheese

METHOD:

  1. Cut cherry tomatoes and olives in halves
  2. Mix with boiled macaroni, fried bacon, onion and grated cheese, but keep a little cheese to psrinkle on top
  3. Place in a greased casserole dish
  4. pour 1/2 cup milk on top
  5. Bake 15 mins in 200 c oven, with lid on and additional 1/2 hour without lid

Notes:

  • Makes enough for 5 big servings served with salad for main course or small serves would be good entree
