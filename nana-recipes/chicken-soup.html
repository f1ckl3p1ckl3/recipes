Chicken Soup

(from Barb)

INGREDIENTS:

  • Chicken stock
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Rice or spaghetti

METHOD:

  1. Remove fat from chicken stock
  2. Add diced carrot, sliced onions (rings nicer if not too big), handful of rice / spaghetti, salt
  3. Bring to boil & simmer for 3/4 hour
  4. Can garnish with parsley
