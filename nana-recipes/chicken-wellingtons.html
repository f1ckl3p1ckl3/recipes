Chicken Wellingtons

Serves 6 (from Barb 06/03/1989)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 3 chicken breasts, cut in half and beat out slightly flatter
  • filo pastry (need 2 sheets per serve)
  • 100 g melted unsalted butter (for brushing pastry)
(for mixture)
  • 190 g melted unsalted butter
  • 6-8 chopped spring onions and 1/2 tops
  • chopped parsley
  • packet of mushrooms or asparagus soup mix
  • 3 rashers bacon or ham finely chopped

METHOD:

  1. Place 1/2 chicken breast in filo pastry, top with 1/6 of above mixture and roll into a parcel
  2. Repeat for all ingredients
  3. Place on a greased tray and brush with melted unsalted butter
  4. Bake at 180 C for 25 mins and serve
  5. Or can cool and keep overnight in fridge and reheat at 150 C for 10-15 mins
  6. Serve with noodles and vegetables or salad
