Birthday Sponge

from Australian Women's Weekly magazine

INGREDIENTS:

  • 4 eggs
  • 3/4 cup castor sugar
  • 1/3 cup plain flour
  • 1/3 cup self-raising flour
  • 1/3 cup cornflour

METHOD:

  1. Grease 2 deep 20 cm or sandwich pans, line bases with baking paper
  2. Beat eggs in large bowl with electric mixter until thick and creamy, gradually add sugar, beat after each addition until sugar is dissolved (~10 mins)
  3. Sift flours together 3 times to aerate and mix flours evenly. Sift flours over egg mixture over egg mixture and use spatula to fold flour into egg mixture
  4. Pour mixture evenly into prepared pans, bake in moderate oven for about 20 mins or until cakes are lightly browned and have shrunk slightly away from sides of pan

Notes:

  • Store in freezer until needed as easier to cut, ice and cream while still frozen
  • Marj Young cut each sponge thru so there were 3 layers of Cream and she iced with passionfruit icing
  • Melt good dessertspoon magarine in microwave and add 8 ozs icing sugar and enough pulp of approx 3 passionfruit to make right consistency
