Toffees

Serves ~18 (from Gina Dalton)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 (level) tsp cream of tartar

METHOD:

  1. Bring all ingredients to a boil, do not stir, and boil steadily for approx 30 mins or until a golden colour
  2. Pour out immediately into paper patty cases and can sprinkle with hundreds and thousands
