Dreamy Fruit Salad

Serves 6 (from Denise Angus M.A.P.S)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 oz butter (1 tbsp)
  • 1 oz sugar
  • 2 tbsp sweet sherry
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small can pineapple pieces
  • 1 can red cherries
  • 1 can mandarin segments
  • 4 oz marshmallows
  • 1/2 pint pure cream (no gelatine)

METHOD:

  1. Put butter, sugar, sherry and eggs with 2 tbsp of the pineapple liquid in top of double saucepan over hot water
  2. Cook until thickened, stirring all the time
  3. Allow to cool
  4. Stir in the drained stoned cherries, pineapple pieces and drained mandarin segments
  5. Add chopped marshmallows
  6. Fold in whipped cream, stand in fridge for 8 hours before serving
  7. Spoon into tall glasses, decorate with a swirl of whipped cream

Notes:

  • Can make the day before
  • Double mixture to serve 10-12 people
