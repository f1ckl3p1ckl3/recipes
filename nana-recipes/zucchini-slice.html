Zucchini Slice

(from Barb)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 375 g zucchini
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 rashers bacon
  • salt & pepper
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 5 eggs
  • 1 cup self-raising flour
  • 1 cup grated cheese

METHOD:

  1. Grate unpeeled zucchini coarsely
  2. Finely chop onion and bacon
  3. Combine zucchini, onion, bacon, cheese, sifted flour, oil and lightly beaten eggs
  4. pour into well greased lamington tin (16 cm x 26 cm)
  5. Bake in moderate over 30-40 mins or until browned (180 C for 35 mins)
