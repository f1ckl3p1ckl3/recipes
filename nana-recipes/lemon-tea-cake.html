Lemon Tea Cake

from Barb 1984

INGREDIENTS:

  • 4 tbsp margarine
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups of self-raising flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • pinch of salt
  • grated rind of 1 lemon

METHOD:

  1. Beat altogheter until well mixed
  2. Pour into greased 7 1/2 - 8 inch square tin or 8 inch (deep) round tin
  3. Bake in oven at 180 C for 40 mins
    4. FOR ICING:
  4. 1/4 cup sugar and juice of 1 or 2 lemons, heated to melt sugar
  5. Pour over cooked cake while still in tin
  6. Let cool - no need to ice
