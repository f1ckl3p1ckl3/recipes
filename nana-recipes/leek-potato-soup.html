Leek & Potato Soup

From Paul Rulj (serves 6)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 leeks
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3-4 medium potatoes
  • 2 pints water or chicken stock
  • 1 cup of milk
  • salt & pepper

METHOD:

  1. Melt butter in saucepan, add chopped onions, soften, then add chopped leek
  2. Add stock
  3. Peel and chop potatoes and add to saucepan
  4. Simmer 1/2 hour to 1 hour
  5. Mash in pot or blender
  6. Simmer slowly for a further 1/2 hour
  7. Add milk but don't boil

Notes:

  • 1 1/2 times recipe and 1 extra cup of water made enough for approx. 10 people
  • Only add enough milk to keep a thick consistency... even 1/2 cup is enough if you've doubled ingredients
  • chopped parsley nice to garnish
