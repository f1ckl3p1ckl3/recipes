Pumpkin Soup

Enough for 5 (from Anne)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 750 g pumpkin
  • 2 large onions
  • 250 g potatoes
  • 3 chicken stock cubes
  • 1 litre (4 cups) water
  • salt & pepper
  • 1 cup cream

METHOD:

  1. Peel and chop vegetables, place in water with chicken stock cubes
  2. Bring to boil and reduce heat, simmer uncovered 25 mins or until vegetables are very soft
  3. Put in blender
  4. Return puree to saucepan, add cream, salt and pepper
  5. Bring to boil, stirring, reduce heat, simmer further 5 mins
  6. Only add cream just before serving, 1 teaspoon runny cream in each plate

Notes:

  • Anne doesn't use cream
  • Can make 2-3 days ahead (bring to boil again, stirring, reduce heat and simmer further 5 mins
  • Can freeze (without cream) up to 3 months.
