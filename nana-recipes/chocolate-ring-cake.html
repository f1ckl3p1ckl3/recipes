Chocolate Ring Cake

from Hostess Course

INGREDIENTS:

  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 2 level tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 cup self-raising flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla

METHOD:

  1. Put in basin and pour melted butter on top
  2. Beat hard for 3 mins
  3. Pour mixture into prepared tin for 40 mins for 180 C
    4. FOR MILK CHOCOLATE FROSTING:
  4. 2 cups icing sugar, 2 tabs butter or marg, 2 tabs cocoa and 1 egg
  5. Blend altogether and beat at high speed until light and fluffy
  6. Decorate entirety of cake, can also decorate with grated milk chocolate or blanched almonds
