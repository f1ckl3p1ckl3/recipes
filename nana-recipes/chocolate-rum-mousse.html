Chocolate Rum Mousse

Serves 8 (from Barb)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 6 ounces sweet chocolate
  • 5 eggs (separated)
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp instant coffee
  • 1 tbsp hot water
  • 1/2 pint cream
  • 2 tbsp rum (not essence)

METHOD:

  1. Melt chocolate in top of double saucepan over hot but not boiling water
  2. Remove from heat and allow to cool
  3. Beat egg yolks lightly in separate bowl and beat them gradually into melted choclate
  4. Flavour with vanilla and coffee dissolved in hot tbsp of water
  5. Beat cream till thick
  6. Stir in rum and fold into choclate mixture
  7. Beat egg whites until stiff and fold inot mixture a little at a time
  8. Pour into a serving dish or small dishes
  9. Chill for at least 2 hours before serving
  10. Whipped cream nice with it

Notes:

  • Doubling mixture serves 12
