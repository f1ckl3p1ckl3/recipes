Sage and Onion Stuffing

(Christmas Day, from Bee Nilson)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 loaves of bread, processed into breadcrumbs in food processor
  • 500 g diced onions
  • 3 melted tbsp margarine or butter
  • 3 tsp powdered sage
  • grated lemon rind and juice

METHOD:

  1. Add onions to breadcrumbs
  2. Cook in microwave and then mince in food processor
  3. Add butter, powdered sage and grated rind of a lemon and the juice
  4. Cook in bar tin in oven at 175 C (fan forced) for 1 hour, covered in aluminum foil
  5. Serve in a dish on Xmas Day with Turkey
