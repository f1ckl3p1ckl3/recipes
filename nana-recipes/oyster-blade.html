Oyster Blade Casserole

Serves 2 (from Mum)

INGREDIENTS:

  • oyster blade for 2 people
  • diced carrots
  • potatoes sliced
  • diced onions
  • gravox
  • 1 1/2 cups of water

METHODS:

  1. Roll meat in gravox
  2. Place in a greased casserole
  3. Add carrots, onions and lastly plenty of sliced potatoes on top
  4. Pour over water
  5. Cook in 180 C oven for 2 hours

Notes:

  • No need to pre-heat oven
