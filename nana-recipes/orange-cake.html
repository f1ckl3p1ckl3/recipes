Orange Cake

from Mum

INGREDIENTS:

  • 6 oz self-raising flour
  • 6 oz castor sugar
  • 3 oz melted butter or margarine
  • 2 eggs
  • juice and rind of 1/2 orange, then make up to 1/2 cup with rest milk

METHOD:

  1. Put all (except butter) into basin and beat 3 mins
  2. Then put butter in and beat
  3. Ice with orange juice added to icing sugar
  4. Bake at 160 C in oven for 40 mins - leave to cool and run knife around ring tin lined with greased paper or 2 bar tins, greased
