Curried Sausages

Serves 4 (from Aunty Marge)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 8 thin sausages
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • potatoes into small quarters
  • diced onions
  • cup of frozen peas
  • 2 heaped tablespoons curry powder
  • 4 1/2 cups water
  • 2 heaped tablespoons cornflour

METHOD:

  1. Cover sausages with water and bring to boil, then drain and turn off heat
  2. In another large saucepan, fry onion in a little oil for 5 mins and stir in curry powder and cook for a few minutes
  3. Skin sausages (skin should come off easily after bringing them to the boil) and cut each into three pieces
  4. Add vegetables and water and simmer for 20 mins with lid on
  5. Dissolve cornflour in a little water and add to curried sausages
  6. Stir and simmer to thicken
