Smoked Oyster Soup

Serves 4 (from Mimi)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 x 16 oz can smoked oysters
  • 1 x 16 oz can oyster soup
  • 1 soup can milk
  • salt & pepper
  • 1/4 pint cream
  • 1 dessertspoon dry sherry

METHOD:

  1. Coarsely chop the oysters and put them with the contents of the soup can in a saucepan
  2. Add 1 coup can of milk
  3. Season to taste and heat slowly so that the flavour of the smoked oysters permeates the soup, but do not boil
  4. Remove from heat, stir in cream and sherry, and serve
