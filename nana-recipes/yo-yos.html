YO YO'S

from Mum

INGREDIENTS:

  • 6 oz butter
  • 2 ounces icing sugar
  • 6 oz plain flour
  • 2 oz custard powder

METHOD:

  1. Cream butter and icing sugar
  2. Add flour and custard powder, sifted well
  3. Mix altogether well
  4. Bake about 15 mins at 170 (fan forced oven)
  5. Roll teaspoons of mixture into balls and put on greased tray
  6. Flatten slightly with a fork
    7. For Filling:
  7. 1 cup icing sugar, 1 teaspoon of butter in a cup and pour a teaspoon or so of boiling water on to melt it a bit
  8. Add vanilla and mix into icing sugar
