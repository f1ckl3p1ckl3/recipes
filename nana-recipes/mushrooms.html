Field Mushrooms

from Eric Wintle (old papa)

How to Prepare Them:

  • Prepare by removing stalks (can leave a little bit of stalk) and peel off the outside skin
  • Wash them well

STEWED:

  1. Place in saucepan with a dessertspoon butter and approx. 1/2 cup of milk, for 1 lb mushrooms
  2. Bring to boil and then simmer for 1/2 hour
  3. Salt to taste
  4. Dissolve a dessertspoon of cornflour in a tablespoon of milk and then stir in with mushrooms to thicken

FRIED:

  1. Fry mushrooms in butter
  2. Serve mushrooms with grills or on hot buttered toast
