Apple Dollop

(from Mrs. Mac, next door neighbour in Charles St, Coburg

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp self-raising flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • vanilla
  • hot cooked apples, not too much liquid

METHOD:

  1. Beat butter and sugar
  2. Add egg and beat again
  3. Add flour and milk
  4. Grease sandwich tin and pour mixture in
  5. Then place hot apples on top before cooking
  6. Cook at once in oven at 180 C for 30-40 mins
  7. Sprinkled with sugar when taken out from oven
