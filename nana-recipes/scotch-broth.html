Scotch Broth/Vegetable Soup

From Suzanne Gibbs (serves 8/prep 10 mins/cook 25 mins)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 500 g lamb neck chops or 3-4 lamb shanks
  • 1/4 cup pearl barley (optional)
  • 1-2 sticks celery, sliced
  • 1 carrot, diced/sliced
  • 1 leek, well washed & sliced
  • 1 turnip, peeled & diced
  • 1 white or brown onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup chopped flat leaf parsley (optional)
  • diced potatoes (optional)
  • salt to taste

METHOD:

  1. Place meat, pearl barley & vegetables in the pressure cooker with 5 cups water and salt to taste
  2. Bring the cooker to low pressure over high heat
  3. Once low pressure has been reached, lift out meat on to a plate and cut meat off bones
  4. Cut meat small and return to the broth
  5. Reheat broth gently, uncovered, adding a little more water if soup is too thick
  6. Stir in chopped parsley and serve in piping hot
  7. Cool and store in fridge overnight and remove fat next day
  8. Can serve with a nice chunk of crusty bread
