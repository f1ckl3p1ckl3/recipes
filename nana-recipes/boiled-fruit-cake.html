Boiled Fruit Cake

from Mum

INGREDIENTS:

  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup sultans
  • 1 cup currants
  • 1 tsp spice
  • 1 cup cold water
  • handful chopped walnuts
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate soda
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup self-raising flour
  • 1 cup plain flour

METHOD:

  1. Put all in saucepan and boil 5 mins
  2. Take off heat and add bicarbonate soda
  3. Mix well, it froths, stand 1/4 hour
  4. Add eggs, self-raising and plain flour to mixture, and mix by hand
  5. Line tin 8'' diameter x 3'' deep with aluminium foil
  6. Bake cake in oven for 1 hour at 190 C
  7. OR can use mixture to make little patty cakes which take about 20 mins at 190 C (makes about 3 dozen)
    8. FOR THE ICING:
  8. 1 tsp butter, pour on 1 dessertspoon boiling water
  9. Mix and add to 3/4 cup of icing sugar, add more water if required and add vanilla to taste
  10. Sprinkle with cinnamon when iced
