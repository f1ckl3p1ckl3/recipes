Melon Balls

(from Barb)

INGREDIENTS:

  • cantelope (about 18 melon balls each for entree)
  • glacé cherry (to serve)
for syrup:
  • 1 dessertspoon dry sherry
  • 1 heaped teaspoon light brown sugar
  • 1/4 level teaspoon ground ginger
  • slightest trace of ground cloves

METHOD:

  1. Put melon balls in dish with airtight lid
  2. Pour syrup mixture over melon and mix through
  3. These can soak all day or be done at the last minute
  4. Shake dish during the day
  5. Glacé cherry on top
  6. Serve with syrup divided on each serving
