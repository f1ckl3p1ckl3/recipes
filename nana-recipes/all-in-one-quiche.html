All in One Quiche

(from Mimi)

INGREDIENTS:

  • 4 ozs cheddar cheese
  • 4 rashers of bacon, chopped or 250-300 g and diced
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • packet frozen spinach
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup self-raising flour
  • salt and pepper

METHOD:

  1. Cook bacon first
  2. Mix altogether and stand for 1 hour
  3. Pour into greased 9'' quiche plate
  4. Bake in oven until set at 180 C for 30-40 mins
